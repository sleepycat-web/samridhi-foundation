About Samridhi Foundation

Samridhi Foundation is a non-profit organization founded in 2011. We are dedicated to empowering communities and fostering lasting change. Empowering the next generation of leaders and changemakers starts here.

Innovative Learning

Nurturing Young Minds

We support children's education through innovative learning experiences, helping them reach their full potential and achieve their dreams.

Healthcare Access

Caring for Communities

Our healthcare programs bring essential medical services to those in need, ensuring that everyone has the chance to live a healthy and fulfilling life.

Building Livelihoods

Supporting Artisans and Craftsmen

Our programs empower local artisans and craftsmen, providing them with the skills and resources they need to thrive and sustain their traditional crafts.

Agricultural Success

Nurturing Farmers' Growth

We stand by our farmers, offering guidance and resources to help them embrace sustainable practices, leading to better yields and a prosperous future.

Moments of Transformation and Hope

Explore the transformative work of Samridhi Foundation through our gallery. Each image captures moments of change, hope, and progress from our community events and outreach programs. Witness the smiles, hard work, and positive outcomes that define our mission of empowerment and transformation.

Testimonials

Samridhi Foundation's tireless work in empowering communities across India is nothing short of remarkable. Their dedication to education, healthcare, and women empowerment has touched the lives of millions, making a profound impact in over 2000 villages and slums. The Foundation’s holistic approach, addressing critical needs and fostering sustainable development, is truly inspiring. We at The Times of India applaud their relentless efforts and unwavering commitment to building a brighter future for all. Their success stories are a testament to the power of community-driven initiatives and the hope they bring to lives.

The Times of India

Samridhi Foundation's initiatives have brought transformative change to some of the most underserved regions in India. By focusing on education, healthcare, and livelihood programs, they have empowered thousands of individuals, providing them with the tools and opportunities to thrive. Their rapid disaster response and grassroots empowerment efforts further highlight their comprehensive and compassionate approach. The Hindu commends Samridhi Foundation for their exceptional contribution to societal upliftment and their role in building resilient communities.

The Hindu
