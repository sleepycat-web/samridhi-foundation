Our Programmes

Samridhi Foundation is dedicated to creating positive change through our diverse programmes. Each initiative is designed to address critical needs and empower communities for sustainable development.

Education

Nurturing children's education, nutrition, and holistic development.

Healthcare

Bringing essential healthcare directly to remote communities' doorsteps.
